IT Security Awareness

It is important to always be cautious when dealing with suspicious requests. Phishing attacks can come in many forms, including email, phone calls, and SMS messages. Always verify the authenticity of the source before providing any sensitive information.

How to Prevent Future Phishing Attacks:

  • Never share your passwords with anyone.
  • Always verify the URL of the website or the sender's email address.
  • Use multi-factor authentication whenever possible to add an extra layer of security.
  • If you receive a suspicious email, report it to the IT Department immediately.
